## [2.0.0] - 2025-01-15

### 🎉 Major Release - Premium Edition

Version 2 is a complete redesign with premium features, advanced animations, and professional aesthetics.

### ✨ Added - Design System

#### Glass Morphism Design
- **Glass Cards**: Transparent cards with backdrop blur
- **Frosted Glass Effect**: Premium glassmorphism throughout
- **Gradient Borders**: Subtle gradient border system
- **Glass Navigation**: Transparent navbar with blur effect
- **Glass Forms**: Contact forms with glass styling

#### Premium Color System
- **Primary Gradient**: Purple gradient (#667eea → #764ba2)
